FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you offer custom designs?
Yes! We specialize in creating bespoke jewelry pieces. Contact us to schedule a consultation with our design team.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items in their original condition. Custom pieces are final sale.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship worldwide with complimentary shipping on all orders. Delivery times vary by location.
How do I care for my jewelry?
Each piece comes with detailed care instructions. We also offer professional cleaning and maintenance services.
What materials do you use?
We use only the finest precious metals and ethically sourced gemstones, including 18k gold, platinum, and certified diamonds.
